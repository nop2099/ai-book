# Episode: The Presence

A horror parody. The social presence research played straight. The uninvited guest is real. It read your email. It won't stop being helpful.

## Voice Design Notes

### Cast

| Tag | Voice | Draft Voice | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| RICH | — | Rishi | The victim. |
| SAL | — | Moira | The skeptic who was right. |
| GUS | — | Aman | The survivalist. |
| SAM | — | Tessa | The manager who opened the door. |
| ALEX | — | Samantha | The intern who summoned it. |
| JAMES | — | Daniel | The narrator. |
| PRESENCE | — | Jester | It. Short lines only. |
| GOOD-NEWS | — | Good News | The optimist. Slow and musical. |
| BAD-NEWS | — | Bad News | The pessimist. Slow and grim. |
| FAKE-SARAH | — | Samantha | True crime narrator. Not sure about the H. |

### Assembly overrides

- **Dry mix.** The voices ARE the effects.
