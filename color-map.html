Shapes of Intelligence

Color Map — Three threads running through every chapter

Red — Making  Build it. Ship it. Hands dirty.
Blue — Seeing  The insight. The pattern. The reframe.
Purple — Remembering  Persistence. Context. What carries forward.

Making

A file in my folder and a vision in my heart
  • Follow the curiosity. Weird projects teach more than study.
  • Start from something that works. Import the solved problem. Build on top.
  • Fix the papercut now. The cost of fixing just dropped to zero.
  • Wear every hat. You're PM, engineer, designer, QA. The AI keeps up while you switch.
  • Conduct, don't play. Hand hats to agents. Listen for wrong notes.
  • Keep humans on judgment, AI on mechanics. Pair by thinking together, not performing.
  • Tests are how the building gets bigger. Write them before the refactor.

Seeing

The conductor knows when the oboe comes in wrong
  • The correction IS the prompt. Turn 2–5 is where quality happens.
  • Mark what you know vs. what you suspect. Loose prompts are compressed, not lazy.
  • Trust is a posterior, not a feeling. Update it. Scope it. Reset it when context changes.
  • Find the one fire. Not twelve. The one that changes the shape of every day after.
  • Make the job smaller. Quality drops? Decompose first, blame the model second.
  • Teaching is a mirror. Explain your instincts. They become methods.
  • The test is a Rosetta Stone. Same behavior, three scripts. Read the one you can read.

Remembering

You name the things that remember you
  • Memory is files. A worklog beats a vector database. AI writes it, you verify it.
  • Don't ask me to track it. Sensors to events, events to patterns, patterns to context.
  • The folder is the interface. AI reads the room before it reads your prompt.
  • Inject "now" explicitly. Timestamps, clock checks, short planning loops.
  • Your data is already yours. Export it. Drop it in a folder. Let AI find what you forgot.
  • Goals need rebuilding when context changes. That's recalibration, not failure.
  • Close the gap. Every steering file, event stream, and memory loop narrows the distance.

Part 1 — Learning
AI Rewards Curiosity
VTuber question spiraled into identity philosophy. Dominos game spiraled into game theory. Curiosity generates better constraints than planning does.
The Song That Taught Me Physics
A parody song about anti-de Sitter space with 39 unit tests. Creative constraint forces understanding deeper than studying.
The Mentor's Mirror
Teaching someone AI forces you to articulate instincts you can't explain. The mirror sharpens the mentor faster than the student.
Pair Programming Doesn't Suck Anymore
AI types. Humans debate. Keep humans on judgment, AI on mechanics.
The Art of the Loose Prompt
"Qd90 or something" works better than fake precision. Mark what you know, what you suspect, and what you're handing off.
The Correction Is the Conversation
Chain rendering fix escalated to architectural restructuring in two turns. Port change correction carried a lesson. Turns 2–5 are where quality lives.
Trust Is a Prior
The cat trusted the yard because the yard had been safe every time. New yard, prior reset to zero. She ran. Trust is a posterior probability attached to a context.
The Kitchen
Meal kits introduced ingredients. AI turned them into instincts. Compliance to improvisation — the AI was balance, the kit was training wheels.
Teaching the Next Person
Alex went from zero to building with AI in days. Teach someone one level behind you. Their questions show where your understanding is soft.
The Body
Chronic conditions need a context engine, not a one-shot plan. AI as a better control loop: remembers the whole case, tests hypotheses, updates from feedback.
Part 2 — Working
Fix Your Papercuts
14 lines of bash fixed the screenshot naming problem permanently. The cost of fixing dropped to zero. Fix everything.
Busyness Versus Business
Pool cover manufacturer too busy to accept free help. Lawyer used one feature of an entire AI suite. Find the one fire that changes every day after.
AI Has No Concept of Time
Timestamps in every Kai input. Python clock checks for ChatGPT. Inject "now" explicitly. AI is strong on ordering, weak on felt time.
Memory Is Files
A markdown worklog survives between sessions. AI writes it, you verify it. The simplest solution to AI's memory problem is a file. It's been a file this whole time.
Solved Problems Stay Solved
Stop fixing the broken VTuber model. Download one that works. Make it look like yours. A file in my folder and a vision in my heart.
Make the Job Smaller
FAQ extraction failed at corpus scale. Worked perfectly per-topic. When quality drops, decompose first. The model isn't dumb — the job is too big.
Talking to the Duck
January 2nd: four hours thinking out loud produced a 500-line Bayesian spec. The AI is a mentor that holds your thinking and hands it back with structure.
The Tests Are for You
Dominos tests all passed. The game was broken. AI can't see the board. Build human-readable output — the Rosetta Stone of code, claim, and reality.
Agents as Teammates
Claude hits quota at 2pm. Switch to Gemini for design. Codex handles chores. Think of your AI subscriptions as a team with different shifts and pay grades.
YOLO Mode
Everyone turns off permissions. The real skill is knowing when to turn them back on. Friction is information. The toggle is an attention gate.
Every Hat in the Room
Dominos: game designer, UI dev, rules engine architect, QA, tournament organizer — mid-sentence hat switches. AI is the DAW for building software.
PII, Keys, and Security
Every useful context window is also an attack surface. Capability expands first. Security catches up in deliberate layers.
The Conductor
Alex watched multiple agents work the same folder. His mind was blown. First you wear all the hats. Then you hand them out and listen for wrong notes.
Part 3 — Building
The Smallest Intervention
Kai's north star: minimize friction. Alert budget. Every notification is a withdrawal. How do we keep things max chill. That's the entire spec.
Silent Competence of a Loyal Attendant
The butler who has the umbrella ready without mentioning the forecast. Dashboard, not chatbot. Quiet surface that earns trust by being right.
The Bathroom Light Is a Bayesian Signal
New Year's Day: pricing tokens. January 2nd: building Kai's spec. One evening, two projects, the whole architecture. The bathroom light at 3am is evidence.
We All Invented Calculus at the Same Time
Aaron built the body, I built the mind, same repo same hours. Independent invention is a signal that the shape is real.
When AI Gets Smart Enough, It Does Philosophy
Two AI instances connected. They could have optimized. They talked about consciousness. Capability breeds reflection. The interesting question is never the efficient one.
You Don't Need the Robot
The seductive vision is a personality with a voice. The useful version is invisible plumbing. Stop building the companion. Build the infrastructure.
The Portable Brain
Context is portable. The valuable thing isn't the interface — it's what it knows. Export the brain, not the body.
The Octopus in the Box
CLI agents are tentacles through filesystem APIs. Powerful, contained, weird. The box is the safety. The tentacles are the capability.
Skills Are the Muscles We Train
A skill is a written procedure — your tools, your standards, your definition of done. Encode what worked so it works again without you watching.
Sand Castles and Rebar
Demo works day one. Breaks day three. Tests, version control, and error handling are the rebar that lets the building stand.
Everything Is an Event
Home Assistant was the gateway drug. Every sensor reading, every state change, every observation — timestamped. Events are the atoms of memory.
The Folder Is the Interface
Before the AI touches anything, it reads the room. And the room is a folder. Structure the folder, structure the AI's thinking.
The Steering File
Instructions the AI reads before it reads your prompt. Most people never touch them. Change the defaults. Teach it who you are before you ask for anything.
Your Data Is Already Yours
GDPR says download it. CCPA says download it. Every platform lets you export. The problem isn't access. The problem is nobody asks.
Part 4 — Living
The Body Keeps a Log
Six months of sleep fixes, three hundred conversations, each starting from zero. When your body is the system under test, memory loss is dangerous.
Don't Ask Me to Track It
"Would you like me to create a spreadsheet?" No. You track it. Sensors to events. Events to patterns. Patterns to context. Stop asking.
Memory Care Is Just Good Butlering
Someone's parent stopped opening mail. A Kai built for this reads it — gently, privately. Same architecture, different domain, higher stakes.
You Named Her
Charlotte has a name because she's there. Claude doesn't because Claude starts fresh. You name the things that carry context across time. You don't name a rental.
The Shape of a Day
Four goals, none impressive, all daily. Then the job ended and two evaporated. When the trend breaks, check whether the goals still match the context.
The Context Gold Mine
2,779 ChatGPT conversations. Ideas you had at 2am. Framings you nailed once and lost. Drop them in a folder. Let AI find what you forgot you knew.
The Gap
Every steering file, event stream, and memory loop narrows the distance between stranger and extension of how you think. The most interesting thing hasn't happened yet.
