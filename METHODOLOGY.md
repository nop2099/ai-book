# Methodology

How this book gets made.

## The Process

This book is built through conversation. James talks with an AI about AI — the irony is part of the point. Ideas emerge naturally through discussion, not through outlining or planning.

## Three Files, Three Purposes

### `book.md` — The Book
Polished entries that have earned their place. Each one captures a single idea with enough context to stand alone. Entries land here only when James says so — "add that," "save that one," or similar. The writing is refined but stays true to the conversational voice where the idea was born.

### `observations.md` — The Raw Log
Timestamped notes on what James is thinking about, captured in real time. No polish, no editing. This is the unfiltered stream — topics raised, metaphors tried, tangents explored. It exists to preserve the texture of how ideas develop before they become "ideas."

### `METHODOLOGY.md` — This Document
A living record of how the process itself evolves. Updated as we discover what works and what doesn't.

## How a Conversation Works

1. James shares anecdotes, metaphors, observations, questions — whatever's on his mind about AI
2. The AI engages: pushes back, riffs, sharpens, connects ideas
3. Throughout the conversation, raw observations are logged with timestamps
4. When something clicks, James flags it for the book
5. The flagged idea gets written up as a polished entry and added to `book.md`

## Principles

- **Voice matters.** The book should sound like James, not like a textbook. Metaphors, informality, and personality are features, not bugs.
- **Raw before refined.** The observations log captures everything. The book captures what survives.
- **Ideas earn their place.** Nothing goes in the book automatically. James decides what's worth keeping.
- **The process evolves.** This methodology doc gets updated as we learn what works.
