[NARRATOR]
The local steering file lives inside a specific project folder. It only applies when you're working in that folder. That's where the bank-specific rules go, or the recipe-specific rules, or the party-favor-specific rules. The local file is the folder's personality. The global file is yours.

[NARRATOR]

If it's specific to this project, you say "add that to my CLAUDE dot md" — the local one, the one in this folder. The rules accumulate from use. The global file grows as you discover things that are true about you everywhere. The local file grows as you discover things that are true about this project. Both of them are residue. [NARRATOR]
The pattern is: experience a friction, correct it in the conversation, then say "add that to my CLAUDE dot md." That's the whole workflow. You don't sit down and brainstorm what might go wrong. You work, you notice, you capture.

[NARRATOR]
It's the same way you refined your baking. You didn't sit down one day and write "the complete guide to Rich's kitchen rules." You baked something. The crust came out wrong. You adjusted the water temperature. Next time, the crust was better. Over time, you built up a body of knowledge about how YOUR oven works, how YOUR water temperature affects YOUR dough, in YOUR kitchen. The difference is: that knowledge lives in your head. The steering file puts it in a place the AI can read. So the AI benefits from your accumulated experience, not just your current session's corrections.

[NARRATOR]
Now let me tell you something that might surprise you. Every conversation you have with Claude — every session — is saved. The full transcript. Every message you sent, every thing Claude did, every file it touched. It's all in a file on your computer. Not hidden. Not encrypted. Just a file. You could open it and read it. It's a list of everything that happened, in order, one line per event.

[NARRATOR]

Your messages are preserved in the file but the AI might forget the details from two hours ago. Which is exactly why the steering file matters. The steering file survives compaction. When the conversation forgets, the CLAUDE dot md remembers.

[NARRATOR]
That's the relationship. Conversations are where discoveries happen. Steering files are where discoveries survive. The conversation is the lab notebook. The steering file is the published finding.
